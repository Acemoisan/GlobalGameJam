Workflow for Custom Textmesh Pro Material Presets.

1. Find a font. 
2. Import TextMeshPro
3. Window > TextMesh > Font Asset Creator
4. Drag in Your Font
5. Create font with "smooth" render. (This will generate the TextMeshPro Font)
6. To generate font atlas (for photoshop editing). Select TMPFont. click on 3 dots > Extract Atlas. (this can be edited in PS)
7. To create a material Preset.  Right click on TMP Component. scroll to material at bottom of inspector. Right Click > Create Material Preset
8. this allows all material presets to be selected, under the preset list, on the TMP component.
